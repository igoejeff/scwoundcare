Our Story & Mission

About Carolina Wound Care

For over 20 years, our board-certified specialists have dedicated themselves to one thing: helping patients with chronic, non-healing wounds finally heal — in our clinics and in their homes across South Carolina.

Our Mission

Wound Care That Meets You Where You Are

Carolina Wound Care was founded on a simple but powerful belief: every patient deserves access to board-certified, specialist-level wound care — regardless of their mobility, location, or situation.

Too many South Carolinians were living with chronic wounds that weren't healing because they couldn't get to the right specialist. We set out to change that — bringing advanced wound care directly to homes, nursing facilities, and assisted living communities across all 46 SC counties.

"We believe that access to specialized wound care should never be limited by geography, mobility, or circumstance. We come to you."
— Carolina Wound Care Founding Principle

What Makes Us Different

A Different Kind of Wound Care Practice

We Come to You — Everywhere in SC

Our mobile care teams travel to all 46 South Carolina counties. No transportation barriers mean better healing outcomes for patients who need it most.

Board-Certified Specialists Only

Every wound care provider at Carolina Wound Care is board-certified in wound management — a credential that requires specialized training beyond standard nursing or medical degrees.

InteliWound Documentation

Our proprietary InteliWound system captures real-time wound measurements, photos, and treatment data — keeping every member of the care team informed and accountable.

Same-Week Access

New patient appointments are typically available within the same week. We understand that wound care delays lead to complications — so we prioritize rapid access.

Medicare & Insurance Accepted

We navigate the insurance process so you don't have to. Medicare, Medicaid, and most major plans are accepted. We verify your coverage before your first visit.

20+ Years of Expertise

Our team brings more than two decades of focused wound care experience. We've treated thousands of patients with complex, chronic wounds throughout South Carolina.
