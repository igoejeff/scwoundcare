How It Works

How Our Mobile Wound Care Visits Work

1

Call or Schedule Online

Call 866-540-8090 or submit an online request. We'll verify your insurance and confirm your home address or facility location.

2

Appointment Confirmed

We typically schedule new patients within the same week. You'll receive a confirmation call with the expected arrival time.

3

Specialist Arrives

Your board-certified wound care specialist arrives with all necessary equipment — supplies, dressings, documentation tools — ready to treat on the first visit.

4

Ongoing Care Schedule

After assessment, we establish a recurring visit schedule — weekly, bi-weekly, or as clinically indicated. Progress is tracked and shared with your care team.
