Core Treatment

Advanced Debridement

Debridement — the removal of dead, damaged, or infected tissue — is the cornerstone of effective wound care. Without proper debridement, even the most advanced dressings and therapies cannot work effectively. Our specialists are trained in multiple advanced debridement techniques tailored to your specific wound.

We perform sharp/surgical debridement, autolytic debridement, enzymatic debridement, and mechanical debridement — selecting the right method based on your wound type, depth, and tissue status.

Key Benefits
  • Removes infected & necrotic tissue blocking healing
  • Stimulates growth of healthy new tissue
  • Reduces bacterial burden and infection risk
  • Can be performed in-clinic or at your home
Advanced Therapy

Specialized Skin Grafts & Biologics

For chronic wounds that are not responding to standard treatment, advanced biological skin substitutes and grafts can be transformative. These products contain living cells and growth factors that actively stimulate healing in wounds that have stalled.

We use FDA-cleared biological skin substitutes, acellular matrices, and other advanced graft products to promote angiogenesis (new blood vessel growth) and tissue regeneration in non-healing wounds including diabetic foot ulcers, venous leg ulcers, and post-surgical wounds.

When Grafts Are Used
  • Wounds not healing after 4+ weeks of standard care
  • Large or deep wounds requiring accelerated closure
  • Diabetic foot ulcers at risk of amputation
  • Wounds with significant tissue loss
Featured Technology

UltraMist® Therapy

UltraMist® is a revolutionary non-contact, low-frequency ultrasound system delivered through a gentle mist of saline solution. Unlike traditional ultrasound, it requires no direct contact with the wound — making it pain-free and safe for even the most sensitive wounds.

The ultrasound energy delivered through the mist activates a cellular healing cascade — increasing blood flow, reducing inflammation, fighting bacteria, and stimulating growth factors that accelerate tissue regeneration.

Ongoing Care

Advanced & Specialized Dressings

Not all dressings are equal. Standard gauze from a pharmacy may actually hinder healing. We use clinically-selected, advanced wound dressings — foam, hydrogel, silver antimicrobial, collagen, alginate, and more — chosen specifically for your wound's needs.

  • Antimicrobial dressings to fight infection
  • Moisture-balancing dressings for optimal healing
  • Collagen dressings to stimulate tissue regrowth
  • Compression therapy for venous ulcers
Patient-Centered

Personalized Wound Care Plans

Every wound and every patient is unique. We develop comprehensive, individualized care plans based on detailed wound assessment, your medical history, nutritional status, and personal goals — then continuously update it as you heal.

  • Complete wound assessment & photography
  • Coordinated care with your primary physician
  • Patient & caregiver education and training
  • Regular progress monitoring & plan adjustments
