Conditions We Treat

Wounds That Won't Heal Are Our Specialty

Chronic wounds need more than standard care. Our specialists are trained for exactly this.

Diabetic Foot Ulcers

The #1 cause of non-traumatic amputation — highly preventable with early specialist care.

Venous & Arterial Leg Ulcers

Lower leg wounds caused by poor circulation that standard dressings can't fix alone.

Pressure Injuries / Bed Sores

All stages treated — from early redness to deep tissue wounds. In-home care available.

Post-Surgical Wounds

Incisions that dehisce or fail to close after surgery need specialized wound management.

Burns & Traumatic Wounds

Complex wounds from burns or injury requiring ongoing specialist care to close properly.

Any Wound Not Healing

If your wound hasn't improved in 2–6 weeks, we can help — regardless of the cause.
