True Mobile Care

We Come to You — Anywhere in South Carolina

Our mobile wound care team is equipped with everything needed to deliver full clinical-level wound care at your home, assisted living facility, nursing home, or wherever you are across South Carolina.

Where We Go

We Come to Every Setting

Our mobile team serves patients wherever they live or receive care — no setting is too challenging.

Private Residences

We provide full wound care visits in the comfort and privacy of your own home — the most convenient option for patients with mobility challenges.

Assisted Living Facilities

We partner with assisted living communities across SC to provide expert wound care for residents who need specialized treatment on-site.

Skilled Nursing Facilities

Our specialists visit skilled nursing and long-term care facilities to treat complex wounds — including pressure injuries and diabetic ulcers — that require more than standard nursing home wound care.

Rehabilitation Centers

Post-surgical and post-hospitalization patients in rehab often have wounds that need specialist management. We bring expert care to you during your recovery.

Adult Family Homes

Small care settings and adult family homes are welcome. We serve any setting where a patient needs wound care and can't easily access a clinic.

Remote / Rural Locations

Don't let rural location prevent you from getting expert wound care. We serve all 46 SC counties, including communities that are far from traditional wound care centers.
