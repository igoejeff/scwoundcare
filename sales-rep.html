Wound Care in Facilities Is Broken — We Fix It

Many patients in skilled nursing facilities, assisted living communities, and home health programs have chronic or complex wounds that require specialist-level care. Yet access to that level of expertise is frustratingly limited.

Standard home health aides and generalist nurses are doing their best — but without advanced training, proper equipment, and board-certified oversight, wounds stall, infections develop, and hospital readmissions climb.

That's where Carolina Wound Care steps in. We partner directly with your facility or team, bringing the full clinical capabilities of a wound center right to your patients — so you never have to choose between quality of care and patient convenience.

What Makes Us Different

The Partner Your Patients — and Your Facility — Actually Need

We're not a call center or a staffing agency. We're a dedicated wound care practice built from the ground up to serve patients where they are — and to make your job easier in the process.

We Come to Your Patients

Our mobile wound care team serves all 46 South Carolina counties. Skilled nursing facilities, assisted living communities, group homes, private residences — wherever your patient is, we come to them.

Clear Communication at Every Step

We use InteliWound technology to share comprehensive assessments, wound measurements, progress photos, and provider orders — giving your team complete visibility into every patient's care plan.

Faster Healing. Fewer Hospitalizations.

Advanced debridement, biological grafts, compression therapy, and specialized dressings mean we close wounds faster — significantly reducing the readmission risk that standard home health care cannot address.

Board-Certified Specialists

Your patients are seen by credentialed wound care specialists — not generalist nurses or aides. Expert providers mean accurate assessments, appropriate treatment selection, and accountability at every visit.

Medicare & Most Insurance Accepted

We handle all billing, prior authorizations, and insurance verification in-house. Medicare Part A & B and most major PPO plans are accepted — at no added administrative burden to your team.

Same-Week Access & 24-Hr Response

Most referrals receive an appointment within the same week. We acknowledge every referral within 24 hours and keep the referring provider in the loop throughout the episode of care.
