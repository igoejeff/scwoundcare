Conditions We Treat

We Specialize in Wounds That Won't Heal

Chronic wounds are different from ordinary cuts and scrapes. They require specialist intervention to heal properly.

Diabetic Foot Ulcers

Specialized treatment for foot wounds associated with diabetes — preventing amputation and restoring mobility.

Venous & Arterial Leg Ulcers

Expert care for lower extremity wounds caused by poor circulation, venous insufficiency, or arterial disease.

Pressure Injuries / Bed Sores

Treating stages 1–4 pressure ulcers in patients with limited mobility — preventing progression and promoting closure.

Post-Surgical Wounds

Wound dehiscence, non-healing surgical incisions, and post-operative complications managed with expert care.

Sacral Ulcers

Specialized management of sacral and coccyx wounds, particularly in elderly or bedridden patients.

Other Chronic Wounds

Any wound that hasn't improved in 2–6 weeks deserves specialist attention. We treat all complex chronic wound types.

Is This Right for You?

Know Before You Call

Here's a clear, honest guide to whether Carolina Wound Care is the right fit for your situation.

We can help you if…

  • Your wound hasn't improved in 2–6 weeks
  • You have diabetes and a wound on your foot or leg
  • You've been told you may need an amputation
  • You have a pressure wound or bed sore
  • You have a venous or arterial leg ulcer
  • Your surgical wound hasn't closed properly
  • You struggle to travel to a clinic
  • You have Medicare or a PPO plan

We are not the right fit if…

  • Your wound is a minor fresh cut healing normally
  • You need emergency or acute trauma care (go to ER)
  • Your wound is actively improving week over week
  • You require inpatient hospital care or surgery
  • You are outside South Carolina with no telehealth need
  • Your primary care physician is already managing it well
  • You have a wound that is less than two weeks old
  • You only need a basic dressing change with no specialist input

Not sure? Call us at 866-540-8090 — we'll tell you honestly whether we're the right fit.
